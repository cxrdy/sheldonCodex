FIFA, also known as the Fédération Internationale de Football Association,
is the global governing body for the sport of soccer (football in most parts of the world).

Founded in 1904, FIFA's mission is to promote and develop soccer worldwide, organize international competitions,
and ensure the integrity and fair play of the sport. One of FIFA's most prominent events is the FIFA World Cup,
a quadrennial tournament that brings together national teams from around the world to compete for the prestigious title of world champion.

With a rich history and a global fanbase, FIFA plays a vital role in uniting nations through the beautiful game of soccer.
